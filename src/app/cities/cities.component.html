What city would you like information on?



Talking about your hometown or a city that is interesting to you is a great way to drive conversation to something you are confortable talking about. Grab a fun fact or two and practice introducing them. "I really like traveling and I'm new to tech. I just read that Berlin has a thriving tech startup community. What has your experience been with traveling?"

Tips

  • Try to ask open-ended questions.
  • Personalize your intro or response. Even if you've never visited
  • Boost your confidence by rehearsing with a few friends or writing notes for yourself. If you don't get the reaction you intended..Dont worry! Regroup and try again.
